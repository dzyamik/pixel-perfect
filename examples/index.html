pixel-perfect

Phaser v4 destructible terrain & pixel-perfect collision. Demo sandbox — open one to verify the library does what it says, or pull a demo apart to learn the API.

  • 01basic rendering live
    Procedural terrain painted from a `ChunkedBitmap` into canvas-backed Phaser textures. No interaction, no physics — just verifies the rendering pipeline.
  • 02click to carve coming
    Click anywhere on the terrain to carve a circular hole. Watch the chunks repaint.
  • 03physics colliders coming
    Same as 02 but with a Box2D world wired up. Drop a sprite and watch it land on terrain that follows the bitmap.
  • 04falling debris coming
    Carve through an island; detached chunks become dynamic Box2D bodies and fall under gravity.
  • 05pixel-perfect sprite coming
    Two sprites with transparent regions; only solid pixels register collisions.
Run npm run dev, open this page, and click a demo. New demos will appear here as Phase 3 ships.
